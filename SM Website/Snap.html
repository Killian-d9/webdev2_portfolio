Snapchat

Snapchat is a popular messaging app that lets the user to send pictures and videos called snaps. Snapchat was founded in 2011 by Evan Spiegel, Reggie Brown, and Bobby Murphy, all students at Stanford University

Uses for college

For anyone wondering how such a social media tool such as Snapchat could be used for college students and lecturers here is how:

  1. Snapchat can be very useful in terms of helping people with their college work. As well as sending pictures and videos, it can also send text, make calls and face-time calls to, if talking directly to someone would be required.
  2. Also on snapchat, there are stories which allow the user to post pictures and vidoes for 24 hrs. Also the user can make private stories which can only be viewed by chosen people.
