Messenger

At most, Messenger is a social networking app which allows the user to send messages, pictures, videos and voice clips with one another. It was a product of Facebook who developed their own messaging app.

Uses for college

For anyone wondering how such a social media tool such as Messenger could be used for college students and lecturers here is how:

  1. Direct messages: Direct messages or DMs allow a person to message one person directly or you can create a group chat and text multiple people at the same time. This would be good for sharing information on an assignment.
  2. Calls can be made using messenger that can make explaining a specific piece of work easier to understand.
