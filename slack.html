Uses for college

For anyone wondering how such a social media tool such as Slack could be used for college students and lecturers here is how:

  1. Slack woould allow a lecturer to create a room dedicated to whatever class they wanted.
  2. The room itself would allow students to communicate with one another if they needed any clarification.
