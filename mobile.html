Using these social media tools on your mobile

The social media tools that I will be showing you today also come with the added advantage of being able to be downloaded as an aplication on your mobile phone. Below are instructions on how to get such applications on your phone.

Firstly you will want to open up your App store or Play store depending on whatever phone you have. Then type in the name of the app you wish to download.

Then after seeing the logo of the app you wish to use, click on it and click download/install.

Now you should have the application on your phone ready to use. If this is your first time using the app you will need to make an account, which I will go over how to do so on each of the pages associated with the specific social media tool.
